About Me

Hi, I'm Julia! I grew up on a cucumber farm in a small town in NC. I moved to Wilmington, NC for college, and ended up sticking around for 12 years. I finally gave up the beach bum life, and moved to Raleigh in 2013 to work for a software company. That brings us to now. I can't wait to begin my new journey with coding!

In my free time, you can find me in the gym! I love weight lifting, and have competed in a few body-building shows. I also enjoy cooking, traveling, reading, eating vanilla almond butter out of the jar, and talking about wanting a puppy.
